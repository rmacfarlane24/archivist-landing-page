Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the initial indexing take?

The initial indexing typically takes 5-10 minutes for most hard drives, depending on the amount of data. Subsequent indexing runs in the background and is much faster.

Is my data safe with Archivist?

Yes, absolutely. Archivist only indexes file metadata (names, paths, sizes, dates) and never accesses or stores the actual content of your files. Your data remains completely private and secure.

Can I use Archivist on multiple computers?

Right now Archivist is one device per user. We're working on implementing cloud storage so that you can port your data across multiple devices linked to your account. For instance, your desktop computer and your laptop. For enterprise clients, get in touch with us to discuss your specific requirements.
